Places to visit in Lebanon

    Why should you come to lebanon instead of other countries?


  • Jeita Grotto

    It is a must to visit Grotto Jeita if you visit Lebanon. It is one of the most impressive and interesting natural sites in the world.


  • Temples of Baalbek

    The Roman Temple is one of the best preserved Roman Temple of the world!Even better than many temples in Rome.

  • National Museum of Beirut

    It helps you so much to understand the rich history of Phoenia. The wonderful sarcophagus of Ahiram, of Achilles, the Ford collection of sarcophagi;
    the Abduction of Europa mosaic, Tyre hypogeum, bull protome, Eshmun Tribune are a few of many interesting objects of this museum.

  • Byblos Castle

    Byblos is rich in history and an interesting place to visit. It is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities.
    Byblos castle has a museum which has some finds from the excavations. The views from the castle are beautiful and you can see the ruins below and the sea beyond.

  • The Shrine of Our Lady of Lebanon

    Our Lady of Lebanon in Harissa is an important shrine and pilgrimage site honoring the Virgin Mary.
    The statue is erected on top of a hill and the views are spectacular. The stairs spiral upwards towards the statue.
